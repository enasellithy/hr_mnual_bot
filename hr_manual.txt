HR Manual (8 Pages Equivalent)

1. Hiring & Recruitment

-   Job requisition approval required before opening any position.
-   HR posts job ads across internal portal and external platforms.
-   Applications are screened based on minimum qualifications.
-   Shortlisted candidates proceed to phone screening.
-   Technical interview conducted by department manager.
-   HR conducts behavioral interview.
-   Final hiring decision requires HR + Manager approval.
-   Offer letter issued after salary alignment and grading.

2. Onboarding

-   New employee must submit all required documents before Day 1.
-   HR creates employee profile in HRIS.
-   IT prepares laptop, email, and system access.
-   Orientation includes company policies, safety training, and role
    overview.
-   Manager outlines 90‑day performance plan.
-   Employee signs Code of Conduct and attendance policy.

3. Leave Management

-   Employees must request leave at least 3 days in advance.
-   Sick leave requires a medical certificate if longer than 2 days.
-   Annual leave balance updates automatically after approval.
-   HR reviews leave trends and identifies potential misuse.
-   Public holidays are auto‑assigned based on location.
-   Emergency leave can be granted by HR in special situations.

4. Attendance & Timesheets

-   Attendance is recorded through biometric or mobile app check‑in.
-   Grace period: 10 minutes in the morning.
-   Overtime requires prior approval from the department head.
-   Timesheets must be submitted weekly by all project‑based staff.
-   HR flags repeated late arrivals for disciplinary review.

5. Payroll Processing

-   Payroll cycle runs from the 1st to the 30th of every month.
-   Salary components include: Basic, Allowances, Deductions, Overtime.
-   HR verifies attendance, leave, and overtime before payroll run.
-   Finance reviews payroll and processes payments.
-   Payslips are generated automatically and sent by email.
-   Salary disputes must be raised within 5 working days.

6. Employee Records

-   HR maintains digital files for each employee.
-   Documents include ID, contracts, certificates, and performance
    reviews.
-   Any change in employee data must be updated within 48 hours.
-   Access to employee data is restricted to HR only.
-   Record retention period is 7 years after employee exit.

7. Performance Management

-   Annual performance review conducted every December.
-   KPI template used across all departments.
-   Mid‑year review required for all employees.
-   Poor performance triggers a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).
-   High performers eligible for bonus and promotion cycle.

8. Offboarding & Exit

-   Employee must submit resignation with a 30‑day notice period.
-   HR conducts exit interview to gather feedback.
-   IT disables accounts on the last working day.
-   Finance settles end‑of‑service and pending allowances.
-   Clearance form required from all departments.
-   HR archives employee records after exit process completion.
